Recipe Box

When The Oregonian approached Planet Argon, it was clear they had reached a limit of flexibility on their platform. Mirroring the print version of their Food Section and complementing the media’s food blog, they had maintained an online directory of recipes that have been curated since 2007 (this meant more than 3,600 recipes). But the online experience was compromised by an inflexible structure, leaving readers with little ability to filter and search for a specific recipe, and an experience that lacked any visual engagement or ability to explore the full potential of the database.
