Screaming Circuits

Screaming Circuits is an Oregon-based manufacturer of quick-turn prototype and short-run ciruit boards. They were the first in their industry to offer instant online quoting and ordering. They’ve built boards that have gone up into space and down into the ocean, and we were thrilled to help them design and build their new web app.

In addition to streamlining the complicated quoting and ordering process into a one-page Ember app, we built the Resource Center, a centralized location for all of their helpful articles, tutorials, and content pages.
