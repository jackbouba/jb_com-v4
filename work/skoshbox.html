Skoshbox

Skoshbox delivers a curated box of delicous Japanese snacks each month. After the service’s popularity surged, their original platform of WordPress and e-commerce plugins simply couldn’t keep up. In late 2014, we started working with Skoshbox to help them relaunch on a new platform.

After a few iterations of design and development, Skoshbox now has a new site with a custom CMS that can handle all of their subscription and shop needs. The new design, in addition to meeting their technical requirements, is fun, versatile, and accommodates their primarily mobile traffic. Vastly better than the initial system, their new platform that will allow them to scale their business in the coming years.
