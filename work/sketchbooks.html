I've been keeping a sketchbook for decades. Sometimes the sketches become larger-scale drawings or paintings, but more often than not they live and die within the sketchbooks. I always hate my drawings, as I find them derivative and amateurish, but after a few years I can look back on them with something resembling appreciation.
