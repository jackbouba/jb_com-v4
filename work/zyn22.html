ZYN22

In 2014, Planet Argon helped ZYN22 launch the first of many studios by designing a full marketing and customer application centered around their objective of transforming mind, body, and soul. Within the application, customers can find a class at their local studio, book a specific bike, learn about instructors, monitor their heart rate performance, shop in the ZYN22 store, and more. Our design process was centered around balancing usability with delight while keeping true to the brand. Bold imagery coupled with a clean layout clarifies the message and makes it easy for customers to meet their goals.
