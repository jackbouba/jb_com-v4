I have been buying ridiculous URLs for a long time. It pains me to think how much I spend each year for these stupid things. Oh geez, I just did the math. Although I don’t yet know what I’ll do with most of these domains, I’m fairly confident that I’ll do something with them. Or I’ll sell them. Speaking of which, why hasn’t someone offered me some serious money for crapfood.com yet? Anyway, here is my site dedicated to:

Absurd URLs that I own.

by Jack Bouba

send Email

crapfood.com

Obviously, this one will be about shitty food. Bad-tasting food or unhealthy food? I’m not sure. I have no memory of buying this one.

haikucuckoo.com

I love writing haikus. This site is going to be extremely simple – it’ll consist of just one page displaying a single haiku. If you refresh the page, it shows a new haiku. Perhaps there will be the ability to ‘share’ the poem.

chickencandyandtofu.com

A disgusting combo, right? This is another one that I have almost no recollection of purchasing. Was I sleepwalking?

wearewack.com

One of the many business names I’ve thought of. The ‘wack’ is actually ‘W.A.C.K’, which stands for We Are Cool Kids. So the URL basically translates to We Are We Are Cool Kids.

eggburps.com

We’re all familiar with the unfortunate reality of egg burps. You eat an egg, especially a hard-boiled one, and you’re bound to get some stinky egg burps. This site is about that.

whyareyoureyesweird.com

This one’s a mystery to me. I’m pretty sure this was a Veronica idea.

mildduds.com

One of my many musical pseudonyms. I’ve also gone by Mr Belvedere’s Stereo and Strengthling. Which reminds me – I should really buy strengthling.com.

americangerbil.com

I think this’ll be a site that sells fancy dolls, like American Girl Dolls. But they’re gerbils. Amercan Gerbils.

toilettegazette.com

This is one of my better ideas. It’ll be a newspaper that you read while on the toilet. Perhaps the stories will be made up, or simply insignificant. I’m not sure yet.

thirdwheel.fun

Self-explanatory. Or just check out toiletnap.com. Also, I just realized I own 2 URLs that contain the word ‘toilet.’
